About Us

Our Vision

Ere Oko foods is a food processing company that grows the food organically and controls every step of the process to give a guaranteed food quality to their customer base. Working in line with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals. SDGs 2, 1 and 11 (Zero Hunger, No Poverty and Sustainable Communities) are our gateway to creating sustainable solutions. We incorporate production of healthy meal options with empowering local farmers and also involving the community in processing the food as a source of sustainable communal income. By empowering farmers, we believe we are helping fight unemployment, while improving the prospects for the education of the children in the community.

What we do

Ere Oko foods is a farm to table organic food company in South West Nigeria. Our team is committed to providing a wholesome lifestyle from Nigeria to the world by producing farm to table food products that have been hygienically processed and packed to perfection. We capitalize on empowering local farmers and safe, efficient processing of farm produce to easy, handy, and ready to use packages.

We are dedicated to a higher standard of quality assurance such that you can get your basic staples in even more nutritious hygienic packs. Empowering local agriculture will in the long run provide us with much healthier organic food for the populace and we become healthier as a nation. Ere Oko as the name implies, wants to bring the profits of the farm, but not just to the farmers or just for the marketers, but for everyone who comes in contact with our brand.

By taking over the processing aspects, we leave our farmers free to grow the best produce and also improve on their agricultural knowledge. Our farmers never fall behind on new farming trends as we routinely introduce modern techniques of food production.

Our Goal

Happy farmers and happy healthy customers are our goal, and we will ensure we work tirelessly towards this goal. We make use of key technology in our processing, without compromise on freshness, quality and nutrition. Our quality assurance team are on ground to ensure that only the best of farm produce ever go into our food.

Partnership

Ere-oko foods is open to partnerships with retailers, whole sale food companies and even supermarkets. As far as our quality standards are being met, we are open to making Ere-Oko foods readily available to as many people as possible. For partnership opportunities, please contact us by sending us a mail or call the under listed phone number.
